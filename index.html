    Welcome to BioStatRx¶

    Hi, I’m Rahul.

    I’m building BioStatRx to make biostatistics feel clearer and more usable—through practical notes, real examples, and hands-on code in R and Python. If you’re working with health data (or learning to), I hope this becomes a helpful place to return to.

    A bit about me¶

    I’m originally from India and currently based in the United Kingdom.

    In 2020, I founded StatisticaHub, an e-tutoring platform where I’ve mentored 500+ international students across mathematics and statistics courses (2020–2025). Teaching has shaped how I explain things: simple language, clean intuition, and focus on what actually works.

    Education¶

    • BSc (Statistics) — University of Delhi, India
    • MSc (Statistics) — IIT Kanpur, India
    • MSc (Medical Statistics & Health Data Science) — University of Bristol, UK

    Open to collaboration¶

    I’m happy to collaborate on projects in:

    • Biostatistics & medical statistics
    • Regression modeling (linear/logistic/Poisson/negative binomial)
    • Survival analysis (Cox, time-dependent covariates, competing risks, frailty, recurrent events)
    • Clinical research analytics + reproducible workflows
    • Education content for health data science

    If you’d like to work together, feel free to reach out.
